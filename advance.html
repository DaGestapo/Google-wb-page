How it works in normal search
  • Enter keywords: Ivan Fedorovich Kruzenshtern
  • Enclose the phrase in quotation marks: "the book of Ivan Kruzenshtern"
  • Insert the OR operator between the words: man OR steamer
  • Put a minus sign before the words: -steamboat, -"the book about the steamboat"
  • Insert two dots between the numbers and specify the unit of measurement: 300..1000 rubles, 1812..1846
